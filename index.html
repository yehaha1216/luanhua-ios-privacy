关于嘟嘎记账与隐私的声明

Privacy Statement for DugaLedger

“嘟嘎记账”是由叶子豪（开发者）为您提供本地记账、统计、个性化显示、可选 AI 记账辅助、可选 iCloud 同步、语音输入、文字识别和 iOS 快捷指令文本导入能力的应用。我们非常重视您的个人信息和隐私保护，会按照法律要求和业界成熟的安全标准，为您的个人信息提供相应的安全保护措施。

“DugaLedger” is provided by Zihao Ye as an app for local bookkeeping, statistics, personalized display settings, optional AI bookkeeping assistance, optional iCloud Sync, voice input, image text recognition, and iOS Shortcut text import. We value your personal information and privacy, and we apply reasonable security measures consistent with legal requirements and mature industry practices.

摘要

Summary

嘟嘎记账无需注册账号。默认情况下，您的记账数据和设置保存在设备本地，我们不会上传到自有服务器，也不会收集地理位置、Android_ID、IMEI、IMSI、MAC地址、OAID、软件安装列表、手机号码、联系人信息、短信、通话记录或用于广告分析的数据。

部分可选功能会在您主动使用或授权后处理必要信息：AI 对话会将您输入的消息及必要账本上下文发送至您自行配置的模型提供商；iOS iCloud 同步会通过 Apple CloudKit 同步数据；语音输入会调用录音和语音识别能力；文字识别会调用相机或存储权限；通知和振动会调用对应系统能力。

DugaLedger does not require account registration. By default, your ledger data and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not upload them to our own servers, and we do not collect location, device identifiers, usage statistics, advertising data, analytics data, or tracking data.

Some optional features process necessary information only after you actively use or authorize them: AI chat sends your messages and necessary ledger context to the model provider you configure; iOS iCloud Sync synchronizes data through Apple CloudKit; voice input, camera/photo image text recognition, notifications, and vibration use the corresponding system capabilities.

1. 我们如何收集和使用您的个人信息

1. How We Collect and Use Personal Information

我们仅在实现本应用功能所必需、基于您的主动操作或系统授权的范围内处理信息。

1.1 本地记账与设置：应用会在设备本地保存收支记录、分类、主页信息卡片和布局、语言/主题/显示、声音/振动、用户导入字体和自定义声音文件等配置，用于提供记账、统计和个性化显示。

1.2 AI 记账辅助：当您主动配置模型提供商并使用 AI 对话时，应用会把您的对话消息、应用系统提示、设备时区和当前时间、自定义人格/规则文本，以及在工具调用中必要的账本记录和分类数据，发送到您指定的模型提供商 API 端点。API Key 和 Base URL 保存在设备本地。我们无法访问您的 API Key、对话内容或账本数据。

1.3 语音、文字识别和快捷指令：语音输入会调用录音和语音识别能力，用于把您的语音转换为 AI 记账消息；拍照识别会调用相机；从系统选择内容识别文字会调用存储权限，并将识别文字发送到正常 AI 消息流程；iOS 快捷指令仅把快捷指令产出的临时文本送入应用。用于文字识别的内容仅在内存中处理，不保存文件路径、缓存副本或附件。

We process information only as necessary to provide app features, based on your active actions or system authorization.

1.1 Local bookkeeping and settings: the app stores ledger entries, categories, home info cards and layouts, language/theme/display settings, sound/vibration settings, and user-imported fonts and audio on your device to provide bookkeeping, statistics, and personalized display features.

1.2 AI bookkeeping assistance: when you configure a model provider and use AI chat, the app sends your conversation messages, the app system prompt, your device time zone and current time, custom persona/rule text, and ledger entries or categories needed by tool calls to the model provider API endpoint you configured. API keys and base URLs are stored locally on your device. We cannot access your API keys, conversation content, or ledger data.

1.3 Voice, image text recognition, and Shortcuts: voice input converts your speech into AI bookkeeping messages; camera capture or photo selection recognizes image text and sends it through the normal AI message flow; iOS Shortcuts only pass temporary text produced by the shortcut into the app. Images are used in memory for recognition only; the app does not save images, file paths, cached copies, or attachments.

To provide features you actively choose, the following third-party or system services may be involved:

iCloud 同步

iCloud Sync

在 iOS 设备上，您可以选择启用 iCloud 同步，将记账记录和分类在登录同一 Apple ID 的设备间同步。启用后：

  • 数据会传输到并存储在 Apple 的 CloudKit 服务中
  • 数据传输和存储均使用 Apple 标准安全措施加密
  • 您可以随时关闭 iCloud 同步，本地数据会继续保留在设备上
  • iCloud 同步默认关闭，需要您主动开启

存储在您 iCloud 账户中的数据受 Apple 隐私政策约束。

On iOS devices, you may optionally enable iCloud Sync to synchronize your ledger entries and categories across devices signed into the same Apple ID. When enabled:

  • Data is transmitted to and stored in Apple's CloudKit service
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using Apple's standard security measures
  • You can disable iCloud Sync at any time; your local data will remain on your device
  • iCloud Sync is disabled by default and must be explicitly turned on by you

Apple's privacy policy governs the handling of data stored in your iCloud account.

AI 服务

AI Services

AI 对话为完全可选功能，需要您主动配置兼容的模型提供商后才能使用。发送 AI 对话时，数据会传输到您指定的提供商 API 端点；我们无法访问您的 API Key 或对话数据。

AI chat is entirely optional and requires explicit compatible model provider configuration before use. When you send AI chat messages, data is transmitted to your chosen provider's API endpoint; we cannot access your API keys or conversation data.

语音识别

Speech Recognition

本应用支持通过语音输入 AI 对话消息，使用设备端语音识别服务。iOS 端使用 Apple Siri 语音识别，Android 端使用平台内置语音服务，OpenHarmony 端使用设备本地语音识别引擎。录音数据在设备端或通过平台标准语音识别管道处理，应用本身不存储或传输语音数据（超出平台 SDK 本身行为的范围）。

The app supports voice input for AI chat messages using device speech recognition services. On iOS, this uses Apple's Siri speech recognition; on Android, it uses the platform's built-in speech service; on OpenHarmony, it uses the device's local speech recognition engine. Audio is processed on-device or through the platform's standard speech recognition pipeline and is not stored or transmitted by the app itself beyond what the platform SDK does.
