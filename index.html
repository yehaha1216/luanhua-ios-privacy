Overview

概述

Welcome to Pennary! We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information.

In short: We do not collect your personal data. By default, all data stays on your device. Certain optional features may involve network communication as described below.

欢迎使用乱花！我们非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了我们如何处理您的个人信息。

简而言之：我们不收集您的个人数据。默认情况下，所有数据保留在您的设备上。部分可选功能可能涉及网络通信，详见下文。

信息收集

乱花无需注册账号，不会将您的数据上传到我们的服务器。我们：

  • Do not collect your location information
  • Do not collect your device information or usage statistics

  • 不收集任何个人身份信息

Data Storage

数据存储

All data you create in Pennary is stored locally on your device by default:

您在乱花中创建的所有数据默认存储在设备本地：

  • Ledger entries and categories are stored in a local database
  • App preferences (language, theme, sound settings) are stored in local preference storage
  • AI provider configurations (including API keys) are stored locally in app preferences
  • By default, data is not uploaded to any server
  • You may optionally enable iCloud Sync — see the iCloud Sync section below
  • 记账记录和分类存储在本地数据库中
  • 应用偏好（语言、主题、声音设置）存储在本地偏好存储中
  • AI 提供商配置（包括 API Key）存储在本地应用偏好中
  • 默认情况下，数据不会上传到任何服务器
  • 您可以选择启用 iCloud 同步——详见下方 iCloud 同步章节

iCloud Sync

iCloud 同步

On iOS devices, you may optionally enable iCloud Sync to synchronize your ledger entries and categories across devices signed into the same Apple ID. When enabled:

在 iOS 设备上，您可以选择启用 iCloud 同步，将记账记录和分类在登录同一 Apple ID 的设备间同步。启用后：

  • Data is transmitted to and stored in Apple's CloudKit service
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using Apple's standard security measures
  • You can disable iCloud Sync at any time; your local data will remain on your device
  • iCloud Sync is disabled by default and must be explicitly turned on by you
  • 数据会传输到并存储在 Apple 的 CloudKit 服务中
  • 数据传输和存储均使用 Apple 标准安全措施加密
  • 您可以随时关闭 iCloud 同步，本地数据会继续保留在设备上
  • iCloud 同步默认关闭，需要您主动开启

Apple's privacy policy governs the handling of data stored in your iCloud account.

存储在您 iCloud 账户中的数据受 Apple 隐私政策约束。

AI Services

AI 服务

The app includes an optional AI Agent chat feature. To use this feature:

本应用包含可选的 AI 智能体对话功能。使用此功能需要：

  • You must provide your own API key and base URL for an OpenAI-compatible LLM provider
  • Your API key is stored locally on your device in app preferences
  • When you send messages in AI chat, the following data is transmitted to your chosen provider's API endpoint:
  • 您自行提供兼容 OpenAI 的 LLM 提供商的 API Key 和 Base URL
  • 您的 API Key 仅保存在设备本地的应用偏好中
  • 发送 AI 对话时，以下数据会传输到您指定的提供商 API 端点：

- Your conversation messages
- The app's system prompt (describing the app's features)
- Your device's time zone and current time
- Your custom persona and rule text (if configured)
- Ledger entries and categories (only when AI tools are invoked to query or modify them)

- 您的对话消息
- 应用的系统提示（描述应用功能）
- 您设备的时区和当前时间
- 您自定义的人格和规则文本（如有配置）
- 记账记录和分类数据（仅当 AI 工具被调用来查询或修改时）

  • We do not have access to your API keys or conversation data
  • You are fully responsible for choosing your AI provider and reviewing their privacy policy
  • AI chat is entirely optional and requires explicit provider configuration before use
  • 我们无法访问您的 API Key 或对话数据
  • 您需自行选择 AI 提供商并审阅其隐私政策
  • AI 对话为完全可选功能，需要您主动配置提供商后才能使用

Speech Recognition

语音识别

The app supports voice input for AI chat messages using on-device speech recognition services. On iOS, this uses Apple's Siri speech recognition; on Android, it uses the platform's built-in speech service; on OpenHarmony, it uses the device's local speech recognition engine. Audio is processed on-device or through the platform's standard speech recognition pipeline and is not stored or transmitted by the app itself beyond what the platform SDK does.

本应用支持通过语音输入 AI 对话消息，使用设备端语音识别服务。iOS 端使用 Apple Siri 语音识别，Android 端使用平台内置语音服务，OpenHarmony 端使用设备本地语音识别引擎。音频在设备端或通过平台标准语音识别管道处理，应用本身不存储或传输语音数据（超出平台 SDK 本身行为的范围）。

Third-Party Services

第三方服务

Pennary does not embed any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. The only third-party services involved are those you explicitly opt into:

乱花不嵌入任何第三方分析、追踪或广告 SDK。仅涉及的第三方服务为您主动选择的功能：

  • iCloud Sync (optional, iOS only) — data synced through Apple CloudKit
  • AI Provider API (optional) — conversation and ledger data sent to your chosen LLM provider
  • Speech Recognition — handled by the device's platform-level speech service
  • iCloud 同步（可选，仅 iOS）——数据通过 Apple CloudKit 同步
  • AI 提供商 API（可选）——对话和记账数据发送至您选择的大模型提供商
  • 语音识别——由设备平台级语音服务处理
